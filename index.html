Intro

I have been enamored by the profound influence that data has on our world, touching every realm, from technology and sports to even the most mundane aspects of daily life. As a child, I vividly recall my grandmother's astute observation of our family's soft drink shop failure being linked to my sister's overuse of onions in the kitchen, despite a lack of comprehensive data analysis. Nevertheless, it was during this period that I experienced some of the tastiest and most abundant food.

As a field engineer, following Huawei's assumption of management for MTN's cell towers in Ghana, They ecountered a persistent issue in northern Ghana where some of the Outdoor Unit (ODU) equipment would regularly freeze and require manual resetting. Upon gathering and analyzing temperature data, more durable ODUs were deployed, resulting in seamless operation and freeing up time for other critical assignments.

At Fly Zipline, I was lucky enough to witness its transformative power, propelling the company to soar as a leader in drone logistics. With data at the helm, Fly Zipline engineered stronger drones, secured lucrative contracts, and streamlined processes to reach new heights.

Embark on a thrilling adventure with me, as we uncover the wonders of data and how it elevates every aspect of our lives! From technology to housekeeping and even taste buds, data adds a touch of richness and flavor. Join me on this journey of discovery and see the world in a whole new light!. Kindly, check out my awesome work.

Work

Data Cleaning and Visualization

There has been much discussion surrounding tech jobs and their reputation for being fulfilling in terms of finance and job satisfaction. Job sites like Indeed and Glassdoor seem to support this notion with daily job postings in the hundreds and benefits such as flexibility.

But is this truly reflective of the real-life experiences of tech workers? These questions and more inspired this project to uncover the truth behind the tech job market

The focus of the survey was on the Data sector. A survey was carried out to gather the data.

This data was cleaned in power querry and visualized to produce an interactive dashbord that help to answer these and many other questions
